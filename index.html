One Year Bible Reading Plan

Join us on a journey through the entire Bible in one year. Each day includes carefully selected readings from the Old Testament, New Testament, Psalms, and Proverbs.

This site follows the ONE-YEAR Bible guide found at Kenneth Copeland Ministries

About This Project

Hi, I'm Ed! As a Christian and software developer, I created this application to make the one-year Bible reading journey more accessible and engaging. The audio features were originally customized for my wife Julia, but I'm excited to share this resource with anyone seeking to read through God's Word in a year.

"Your word is a lamp for my feet, a light on my path." - Psalm 119:105
